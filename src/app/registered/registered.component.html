Get Started


Declare your Id

  • Navigate to your CS:GO Directory (eg C:\program files (x86)\steam\steamapps\common\Counter-Strike Global Offensive)
  • Create a new file: settings.dat or click here to download (move to csgo dir)
  • To view your Id, click
  • Copy and paste this line below in your config file. Your config should look like this
    id={{Id}}

Inject the skinchanger

  • Create a new folder named at C:\Windows (eg C:\Windows\Skinchanger)
  • Download SkinChanger.zip click here to download
  • Extract the SkinChanger.zip and place the skinchanger.dll and injector.exe in your new folder
  • Start CS:GO and run the injector
